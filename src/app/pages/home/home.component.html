CaaSCasE

The Comic Sans of Case Types

CaaSCasE or 'Case as a Service Case' is new Case type designed to be a casual/fun case type.

The rules are pretty simple. Both the first and last letter of a word are capitalized.

Tools

Case Type Checker

Detects and tells you the type of case that you are using.

Case Type Validator

Reads your text and tells you if your case type is valid!

Case Type Converter

Converts your text to any case type you want!
